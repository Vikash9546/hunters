Solar Energy

Energy from the sun

The sun has produced energy for billions of years and is the ultimate source for all of the energy sources and fuels that we use. People have used the sun's rays (solar radiation) for thousands of years for warmth and to dry meat, fruit, and grains. Over time, people developed technologies to collect solar energy for heat and to convert it into electricity.

Solar energy and the environment

The Sun is the ultimate source of solar energy, powering life on Earth and driving one of the cleanest, most sustainable forms of energy production. Solar energy harnesses the Sun's abundant and renewable light and heat through advanced technologies like solar panels and thermal systems. This natural power source reduces our reliance on fossil fuels, curbs greenhouse gas emissions, and promotes a greener planet. By capturing even a fraction of the Sun's immense energy, we can fuel homes, businesses, and industries while safeguarding the environment for future generations. The Sun's importance in enabling this energy transformation makes it a cornerstone of sustainable development.
